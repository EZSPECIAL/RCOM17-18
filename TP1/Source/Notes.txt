Behavior:

llopen() Transmitter sends SET, tries up to TIMEOUT times, aborts if error or exceeded TIMEOUT
llopen() Receiver waits for SET, sends UA, doesn't retry and assumes success

llopen() Both BCC1 and Control field are checked for the expected value
